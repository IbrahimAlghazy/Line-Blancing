A Practical Example to Understand Line Balancing

And Never Forget It Again!

Line Balancing: Organizing the production line so that the workflow moves as efficiently as possible - with high productivity and minimum waste.
Bottlenecks: The slow points in the production process that cause delays and reduce overall efficiency.
Takt Time: The time you should spend to produce one unit based on customer demand.
Cycle Time: Actual time it takes to complete a task or produce one unit in the process.
